Honkai Star Rail

Honkai: Star Rail is a free-to-play, turn-based strategy role-playing game (RPG) developed and published by miHoYo (now rebranded as HoYoverse). It is part of the Honkai series, which also includes titles like Honkai Impact 3rd. The game was released in April 26, 2023. Honkai Star Rail feature several characters from Honkai Impact 3rd as returning figures or expies (short for "exported character") such as Welt Yang, Himeko, Bronya, Seele, Sushang, Natasha, Silverwolf, Luocha, and Acheron.
